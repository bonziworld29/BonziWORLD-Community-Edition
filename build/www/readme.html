BonziWORLD Community Edition

The BW fork made by the community, for the community. Leading the industry in literally any chat client.

What the hell is this?

BonziWORLD is a node.js and socket.io based chat client featuring everyone's most hated loved purple gorilla. The client also uses speak.js to provide text-to-speech voices. It's not the exact BonziBUDDY voice, but Hey, it works.

It also uses Windows XP design as 'inspriation'. Also, A couple amount of people are working on this version of BonziWORLD based off an old project named BonziWORLD Revived, which eventually evolved into BonziWORLD Community Edition after 3 or 4 years of age.

How do I use it?

  1. Enter a nickname (under 25 characters). If you don't enter one, you'll be named "Anonymous". (we r legion du nut 4 get)
  2. Optionally, enter a room ID. If you want to join someone else, their room ID will be in the bottom-right corner of the screen.
    • If you enter a room ID that doesn't exist, you will be placed in a private room which can only be joined by people you share the ID with.
  3. Be a BonziBUDDY.

Are there any rules?

Obviously no illegal shit. On top of that, there are a few limitations:

  • All strings are sanitized to get rid of any malicious HTML/JS/CSS. Don't even try it motherfucker.
  • There is a 2500 character limit in public rooms and a 5000 limit in private rooms.
    • Yes, the Navy Seals copypasta fits.
  • There is a 25 character limit on names.
  • A max of 8 people are allowed in a public lobby. After that, a new public room will be created. A max of 30 people are allowed in private lobbies.
    • (Yeah, I know I changed the limit down from 15. With the limited screen space and processing power on mobile, this was part of the reason things were running so shitty. However I have recently undone this change and made the server carry 15 users again.)

Remember that people can play YouTube videos whenever they want. Keep your volume down, headphone users. Chat rooms are for the most part unmoderated. If shit starts getting chaotic, I might start using my banhammer. There are also some filters to prevent BozoWORLDers from advertising their shock site, but it can be wonky. You might disconnect, or something might change.
